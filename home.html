Universal Framework to Benchmark Cryptographic Algorithms

In today's digital world, security is everything. Cryptographic algorithms help protect our data, but how do we know which one is the best for different situations? This framework provides a way to test and compare various encryption and decryption algorithms based on different factors like performance, strength, and efficiency.

Encryption & Decryption Algorithms

AES-CBC

Advanced Encryption Standard in Cipher Block Chaining mode.

ChaCha20

A secure stream cipher used in various applications.

AES-GCM

AES in Galois/Counter Mode, widely used for secure communications.

ChaCha20-Poly

Combines ChaCha20 and Poly1305 for authenticated encryption.

Kyber and AES-GCM

A hybrid approach combining lattice-based cryptography with AES-GCM.

Kyber and ChaCha20

Combines quantum-safe encryption with a fast stream cipher.

Benchmark Categories

Benchmark Based on Media Types

Test the performance of algorithms across different media types.

Benchmark Based on Processors

Analyze performance across different processor architectures.

Benchmark Based on Strength

Evaluate the strength of algorithms under various attack scenarios.
