Universal Framework to Benchmark Cryptographic Algorithms

In today's digital world, security is everything. Cryptographic algorithms help protect our data, but how do we know which one is the best for different situations? This framework provides a way to test and compare various encryption and decryption algorithms based on different factors like performance, strength, and efficiency. Whether it's AES, RSA, or SHA, this tool helps analyze them across different media types and processors, making it easier to choose the right one for your needs.
